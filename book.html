From a jail cell to the pulpit, Rev. Dr. Mancil Carroll III shares a true story of redemption and purpose. Hope for anyone who feels beyond God’s reach—you don’t have to be flawless to be faithful.

About the Book

Blending raw testimony with biblical insight, this book shows how God uses imperfect people to fulfill His perfect will. From an expungement that mirrored the gospel’s promise of forgiveness to reclaiming lost years through education and ministry, Dr. Carroll’s story proves scars are not disqualifications—they are credentials of survival.

Who it's for

  • Readers seeking faith-filled hope and restoration
  • Church groups, men’s ministries, and recovery ministries
  • Leaders discipling others through failure and redemption
